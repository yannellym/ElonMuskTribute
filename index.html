Elon Reeve Musk

Business Magnate

Information about Elon Musk's life and journey:

  • 1971 -> Elon Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa.
  • 1995 -> Started an internet city guide web software company Zip2 with his brother Kimbal.
  • 1999 -> Elon Musk cofounded X.com with $10 million coming from the sale of Zip2.
  • 2002 -> Elon Musk founded SpaceX with $100 million USD of his own project becoming CEO and CTO.
  • 2004 -> Elon Musk joined Tesla's board of directors as Chairman leading the Series A round of investment.
  • 2014 -> Elon Musk announced open-sourcing Tesla technology allowing anyone to use them.
  • 2016 -> Elon Musk revealed plans of colonizing Mars.
  • 2019 -> Elon Musk went to China for the groundbreaking of the new Tesla Gigafactory Shanghai.
